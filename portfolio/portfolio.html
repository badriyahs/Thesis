temporal exploration

class: methods as practice, practice as methods colloquium.
professors: violet whitney and william martin

i was interested in the idea of time as a material, and how it can be manipulated through architecture. the project began with collecting data from my peers about how time is perceived in our studio space.

then the project was a deep dive into the history of timekeeping devices, and the politicization of each.
